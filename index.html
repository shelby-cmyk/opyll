Questions

Honest answers.

How much does Opyll cost? +
Core: $149/mo for compounded semaglutide on a 3-month plan ($199/mo for tirzepatide). Prefer no commitment? Monthly billing is $199/mo sema, $249/mo tirz. Add the full Opyll Stack for $79/mo (Complete tier, $228/mo for sema). Stack SKUs also available a la carte. No insurance required, cancel anytime.
What if I'm allergic to ingredients in the Opyll Stack? +
The Stack is completely optional. Start with our Core tier (just the medication and your physician) and add Stack SKUs only if you want them — and only the ones that fit your diet, allergies, and preferences. Full ingredient lists ship with every product so you can review before opening. The Stack is a wellness offering separate from your physician's clinical prescription.
What medication will I receive? +
Compounded semaglutide or tirzepatide, prescribed by your physician based on your medical history and goals, and prepared by U.S.-licensed 503A compounding pharmacies. Shipped temperature-controlled, monthly, with ongoing clinical support. Compounded medications are not FDA-approved.
What makes Opyll different? +
Competitive entry pricing — $149/mo for compounded GLP-1 on a 3-month plan (or $199/mo monthly, your choice) — paired with the only branded supplement system designed specifically for GLP-1 members. Most programs give you only the medication or force-bundle everything. Opyll lets you start small, scale up with the Opyll Stack when your body asks for it, or skip the Stack entirely. Same active molecule as Ozempic® and Wegovy®, at a fraction of the cost.
Can I just buy the Opyll Stack without the medication? +
Yes. Each Stack SKU — Rise, Daily, Hold, and Flow — is available standalone. The Stack is a wellness offering separate from clinical care.
Do you accept insurance? +
No — Opyll is cash-pay. We keep entry pricing at $149/mo (3-month plan) or $199/mo monthly — specifically to be competitive with what most members would pay in insurance copays plus the prior-authorization friction. If you'd like to use insurance for medication separately, your physician can write the prescription.
I'm new to GLP-1. Is Opyll right for me? +
Yes — Opyll is built for both new starters and existing GLP-1 users. New members get the cheapest physician-led entry point ($149/mo on a 3-month plan) plus the Opyll Stack, which is designed to make the first weeks easier: B-vitamins for energy, Flow for the GI side-effects that derail most starters, and Hold to protect muscle from day one. You don't have to add the Stack — but it's why most members start with the Complete tier.
What if I'm already on a GLP-1? +
You can transfer in at any time. Take the assessment, share your dose and timeline, and your physician will pick up where you are. Want just maintenance support without continued medication? Add the Stack a la carte.
